# 📊 Sales Dashboard (Excel Project)

## Overview

This project is an interactive Sales Dashboard built using Microsoft Excel to analyze sales performance across regions, products, and time. It enables users to identify trends, compare performance, and generate actionable business insights through dynamic visualizations and filters.

## Dataset

The dataset contains 300 sales records with the following fields:

* Order Date
* Region (East, West, North, South)
* Product (Printer, Monitor, Scanner, Keyboard, Mouse, Speaker)
* Sales Amount

Note: This dataset is created for learning and demonstration purposes.

---

## Detailed Insights

### Overall Performance

* Total revenue generated: ₹14,39,670
* Sales distribution is relatively balanced across regions with slight dominance from top regions

### Regional Analysis

* South region contributes the highest share of total revenue
* West region follows closely as a strong performer
* East and North regions show comparatively lower performance, indicating growth potential

### Product Performance

* Printer is the top-performing product and contributes the largest share of revenue
* Monitor and Scanner act as secondary contributors
* Keyboard, Mouse, and Speaker have low contribution and may require improvement strategies

### Time-Based Trends

* Sales peak during July–August, indicating strong mid-year performance
* April records the lowest sales
* Overall trend shows fluctuating monthly performance with a clear seasonal pattern

### Business Insights

* Revenue is concentrated in a few high-performing products, creating dependency risk
* Strong regions (South and West) can be targeted for further expansion
* Low-performing products may benefit from pricing or marketing adjustments
* Seasonal peaks can be leveraged for promotions and inventory planning

## How to Use

1. Download the Excel file
2. Open it in Microsoft Excel (Excel 2016 or later recommended)
3. Use slicers to filter by Date, Region, and Product
4. Analyze KPIs and charts dynamically
